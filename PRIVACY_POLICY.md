# Privacy Policy — One-Click TikGem

_Last updated: June 12, 2026_

One-Click TikGem ("the extension") is a browser extension that captures the
current web page's title and description and adds it as a task to your own
TikGem task manager at https://tikgem.pplx.app.

## What the extension accesses

- **The page you choose to capture.** When you click the toolbar button, press
  the keyboard shortcut, or use the "Add page to TikGem" right-click menu item,
  the extension reads the active tab's title and meta description (and, for the
  right-click action, an optional text selection or link).
- **Your TikGem tab.** The extension opens or reuses your TikGem tab to add the
  task and to read your list names so you can pick a target list.
- **Your settings.** Default tags, status, context, due date, target list, and
  the close-tab toggle are stored in your browser's Chrome sync storage.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer
  or any third party.
- It does **not** include analytics, tracking, advertising, or remote code.
- It does **not** read pages in the background — it only reads a page when you
  explicitly trigger a capture.
- It communicates with **no servers other than your own TikGem app**
  (tikgem.pplx.app).

## Where your data goes

The captured page title and description are sent only to your own TikGem app,
exactly as if you had typed the task yourself. Your settings remain in your own
Chrome profile via Chrome sync storage and are never sent anywhere else.

## Data retention and deletion

The extension keeps no data of its own beyond your settings in Chrome sync
storage. To delete your settings, use "Reset to defaults" on the options page or
remove the extension. Tasks you create live in your TikGem app and are managed
there.
